WG VISITOR PARKING RULES AND REGULATIONS: (upd 5/23/25;Scroll down to send text msg to register your vehicle)

  • To ensure fair and equal opportunity to use visitor parking spots by all rsients and their guests, permitted PARKING TIME IS LIMITED TO 24 HOURS per each QR-code-scan. After 24 Hrs expired, within a 20-minute grace period, the driver may extend the parking time by repeating QR scan/ Text — otherwise, he has to vacate the parking spot.
  • OVERSTAY-PARKING with no scan within the grace period will be charged $30 and the vehicle will be subject to towing.
  • EVERY CONDO UNIT HAS A CREDIT FOR 8 x 24 HOURS SHARED FREE PARKING TIME PER CALENDAR MONTH! Not transferable to the next month or a different unit.
  • EXCESS-PARKING days by any and all vehicles will be charged at $15 per day. A scan is required for every day.
  • VEHICLES PARKED WITHOUT QR CODE SCAN AND TEXT MESSAGE WITH REQUIRED INFO WILL BE TAGGED FOR VIOLATION AND TOW SERVICE WILL BE CALLED.
  • The Association reserves the right to use all legal means of immobilizing or bracing the vehicle while on the Association's property until fees are collected in full.
  • Vehicle operators must agree to all rules above and click the button below to open your default texting application and SEND PREPOPULATED MESSAGE, ADDING HOST'S BUILDING AND CONDO NUMBER; VEHICLE COLOR, MAKE, MODEL AND LICENSE PLATE NUMBER (OR SNAP VEHICLE'S END PHOTO W PLATE) to register parked vehicle with WG Association. IF YOU DO NOT AGREE OR FAIL TO SEND TEXT WITH REQUIRED INFO, You have to REMOVE YOUR VEHICLE OR IT WILL BE REMOVED AT OWNERS EXP. Thank You for your cooperation!
